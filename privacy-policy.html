🍪 Cookies and Similar Technologies

Like most online services, we and our partners use cookies and similar technologies to provide and personalize the Service, analyse use, target advertisements and prevent fraud.

We and our partners collect and store information about users' interactions with unaffiliated websites and applications that use our technologies. This allows us to:

  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Allow users to use the Service on one device and pick up seamlessly on another
  • Analytics, personalization and attribution
  • Limit the number of times a user is shown the same advertisement
  • Provide personalized advertising on each device

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some parts of the Service may then not function properly.

🔒 How We Protect Your Data

Security Safeguards

We are continuously developing and implementing administrative, technical and physical security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or against loss, misuse or alteration.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you the Service. We may periodically de-identify unused game accounts and regularly review and de-identify unnecessary data.

If you ask us to remove your personal data, we will retain your data as necessary for our legitimate business interests, such as to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

👶 Children

Protecting children's privacy online is very important to us. Generally, our Services are not directed to children under 13 (or other age as required by local law), and the Services do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

For games that may appeal to children, we take additional steps to limit access based on the player's age. When a player indicates they are under 13, we will limit their access to certain features, such as social media and chat features.

If you are a parent or guardian and wish to review personal data collected from your child, or have that information modified or deleted, you may contact us as described above.
